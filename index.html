FAQ

Common Questions

intelEstate scrapes official Polish court and bailiff (komornik) portals every night. Data is sourced directly from public judicial databases — not third-party aggregators.
New auctions typically appear within 24 hours of being published on court portals. PRO subscribers get early alerts for auctions within 3 days of their date.
Each auction is scored 0–100 based on three factors: discount vs estimated value (60% weight), auction round — second rounds are cheaper (20% weight), and urgency — days remaining (20% weight). HOT deals score 85+, GOOD deals score 70–84.
Currently we cover all 16 Polish voivodeships, with strongest coverage in Małopolska, Mazowieckie, Dolnośląskie, and Śląskie. EU expansion to Czech Republic and Hungary is planned for 2026.
